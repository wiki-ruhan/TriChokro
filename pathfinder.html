The Crisis

Unsafe electric rickshaws flood Dhaka's streets, causing accidents and environmental hazards. We've identified three critical failure points.

Safety Risks

  • Missing brake systems
  • High center of gravity (tipping hazard)
  • Poor suspension causes injuries

Engineering Flaws

  • High aerodynamic drag
  • Low-capacity wheels
  • Inferior structural materials

Environmental Impact

  • Non-recyclable toxic batteries
  • Industrial waste from imports
  • Noise pollution from outdated motors
02.

The Solution

Engineering excellence meets local innovation.

BUET Innovates

McPherson Suspension

Superior ride comfort and stability compared to conventional leaf springs.

Disc Brakes

Modern hydraulic disc brakes replace dangerous rubber pad brakes.

Aerodynamic Design

Optimized hood and body to reduce air resistance and boost battery efficiency.

Eco-Sustainable

Recyclable components and battery technology that's environmentally safe.
