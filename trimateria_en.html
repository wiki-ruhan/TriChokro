Nature's Alternatives

Materials of Tomorrow

Replacing synthetic polymers with abundant, regenerative organic resources found right here in Bangladesh.

Mycelium

The root structure of mushrooms. It acts as a natural, self-assembling glue that binds agricultural waste together without toxic adhesives.

  • 100% Compostable
  • Fire & Water Resistant
  • Grows in 5-7 days

Banana Fibre

Extracted from the pseudo-stem of banana plants, a massive agricultural byproduct in Bangladesh. These fibers provide incredible tensile strength.

  • High Tensile Strength
  • Lightweight
  • Biodegradable Reinforcement

Cork & Jute

Cork adds elasticity and impermeability, while Jute (the Golden Fiber) provides structural integrity, creating a perfect plastic alternative.

  • Elastic & Flexible
  • Locally Sourced (Jute)
  • Naturally Insulating
